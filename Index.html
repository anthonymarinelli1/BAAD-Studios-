About

WHAT IS BAAD STUDIOS?

We throw Muay Thai fight nights that don't feel like fight nights. We blend authentic Thai combat culture with DJ sets, nightlife energy, and limited-run streetwear from our in-house brand Molly Mondays. Every event is a one-night-only experience — the fights are real, the merch won't drop again, and the crowd is there because they actually care.

Born in Costa Mesa, built on respect for the art. We partner with the best Muay Thai gyms in Orange County to bring real amateur fighters to a stage they deserve. The Wai Kru ceremony opens every card. The after-party closes every night. No corporate sponsors, no watered-down culture — just combat, community, and a good time.

The Night

THE FIGHT NIGHT
EXPERIENCE

This is more than a fight card. From the opening ceremony to last call, every moment is built with intention. We honor the tradition. We bring the energy. Here's how it goes down.

01
6:00 PM

DOORS OPEN

The energy starts at the door. Flash photography, thick bass, and the smell of Thai food trucks lining the venue entrance. Grab your merch at the Molly Mondays booth before your size sells out. This is the only place it drops.

02
7:00 PM

WAI KRU CEREMONY

Before anyone throws leather, we honor the tradition. The Wai Kru Ram Muay is the sacred pre-fight ritual where fighters pay respect to their teachers, their camps, and the art itself. The room goes quiet. This is real.

03
7:30 PM

THE FIGHTS

Eight to ten bouts of amateur Muay Thai. Elbows, knees, clinch work, the whole toolkit. Our fighters come from the best gyms in Orange County and they show up hungry. Ringside VIP puts you close enough to feel it.

04
10:00 PM

DJ SET + AFTER PARTY

When the last bell rings, the party starts. Thai trap, EDM, hip-hop — our DJs read the room and keep the floor packed. Drinks are flowing, the energy is still electric, and nobody wants to leave.

A Note on Tradition

THIS IS MUAY THAI. WE HONOR THAT.

Muay Thai is the Art of Eight Limbs — a discipline with centuries of history rooted in Thai culture and spiritual practice. Every BAAD event opens with the Wai Kru Ram Muay, the sacred pre-fight ritual that connects fighters to their teachers and the traditions that came before them. We host a party, but the foundation is respect.

The Story

ABOUT BAAD

We started BAAD Studios because nobody was doing Muay Thai events the way they deserve to be done. Not in OC, and not anywhere close. We saw sterile fight cards in empty gyms and overcrowded club nights with no soul. We wanted both — the real fights and the real party — held together by respect for the art and a crowd that gives a damn.

BAAD Studios is headquartered in Costa Mesa, California. We produce Muay Thai fight nights that blend authentic Thai combat culture with DJ sets, food and drink, and exclusive merch drops from our in-house brand Molly Mondays. Every event is a one-night-only production — real fighters, limited merch, and an experience built from scratch.

We partner with the best Muay Thai gyms across Orange County. We open every card with the Wai Kru ceremony. We close every night with the speakers still ringing. If you've been looking for something that actually respects the culture while throwing a party worth remembering — you just found it.
